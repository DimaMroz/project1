Homer Simpson:
Occupation: Safety Inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant
Personality: Lazy, bumbling, but lovable father of the Simpson family. Known for his catchphrase, "D'oh!"

Marge Simpson:
Occupation: Homemaker
Personality: Patient, nurturing, and the voice of reason in the family. She has tall blue hair and is known for her distinctive voice.

Bart Simpson:
Age: 10
Personality: Mischievous, rebellious, and often a troublemaker. He is known for his catchphrase, "Eat my shorts!" and is the eldest son of the family.
